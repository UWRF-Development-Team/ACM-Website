Accessing UWRF Tutoring Services

  1. Visit https://students.uwrf.edu/studentsuccess/tutoring-services
  2. Click the “Schedule an Appointment” button
  3. Locate the widget to make an appointment
    1. Find yourself in the “For Student” dropdown, pick the appropriate class, and pick a reason
  4. Click the search button, and click on any available appointments
    If you’d like a different time, click the “Load more for…” button
    1. If there are no available appointments, please click the “Report Unable to Find Appointment” button
  5. Scroll down, and click the “Save” button to finalize your appointment
    1. Please leave additional notes for the tutor if you can, like what you are currently working on, and anything else that may be out of the ordinary
