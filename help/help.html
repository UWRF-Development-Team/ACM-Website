Need help? We've got you!

Campus life is hard... And sometimes, so is getting help.
We at UWRF ACM are willing to help with any CIDS related issues!

How do I get help?

  • Walk in! If the ACM door (South Hall 21) is open, feel free to come in and ask any questions about your courses.
    While we aren't accredited by the university, we will help the best we can and point you in a better direction if not.
  • Access tutoring services! UWRF has tutoring services available, and most people find them very helpful.
    You can schedule an appointment here! If you'd like a walkthrough on how to make an appointment, click here.
  • We understand that sometimes you need help in a different way. We're just students, and we can't help in every way.
    If needed, please access the counseling services here.
    UWRF has many other services it offers that may also help. A full list can be viewed here.
